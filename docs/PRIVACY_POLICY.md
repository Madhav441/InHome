# Sentinel AU privacy summary (draft)

Sentinel AU Pty Ltd (ACN pending) provides family online safety tools. We collect limited telemetry metadata from managed
profiles with verifiable parental authority. Data is hosted in AWS ap-southeast-2 unless self-hosted.

## Collection

- Device identifiers, guardian accounts, policy selections.
- Telemetry metadata (timestamps, URLs/domains, app identifiers, notification titles).
- Classifier outputs (risk scores) and alert dispositions.

## Use

- Enforcing policies, generating alerts, providing reports.
- Improving detection accuracy with de-identified aggregates.

## Disclosure

- No sale of personal information. Third-party processors limited to hosting, email delivery, and support ticketing.

## Rights

- Access, correction, and deletion via privacy centre.
- Complaints: privacy@sentinel.au or OAIC (www.oaic.gov.au).
